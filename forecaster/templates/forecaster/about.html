Our Story

Born from a frustration with noisy, unreliable market data

Markex was founded by a team of quantitative analysts and software engineers who were tired of piecing together market insights from a dozen different fragmented tools.

We built Markex to be the single professional dashboard that combines live market data, XGBoost-powered forecasting, and a full suite of financial calculators — all in one clean, focused interface.

Today, Markex covers crypto, equities, commodities, ETFs, and forex — giving every type of investor the intelligent edge they deserve.
